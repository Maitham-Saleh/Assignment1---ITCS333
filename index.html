Teacher Information

Ms. Sarah Johnson

Ms. Sarah Johnson is a dedicated kindergarten teacher with over five years of experience in early childhood education. She has a passion for nurturing young minds and creating a fun, engaging learning environment. Ms. Johnson believes in the importance of hands-on activities and play-based learning, which helps her students develop a love for learning while building essential skills. She holds a Bachelor’s degree in Early Childhood Education and is committed to fostering creativity, curiosity, and confidence in her students.

Mr. David Lee

Mr. David Lee is a dynamic kindergarten teacher known for his innovative teaching methods and strong connection with his students. With a background in child psychology, he understands the developmental needs of young children and tailors his lessons to meet those needs. Mr. Lee emphasizes the importance of social skills and emotional development, incorporating collaborative activities that promote teamwork and communication. His enthusiasm for teaching inspires his students to explore and discover the world around them.

Ms. Emily Patel

Ms. Emily Patel is a compassionate and experienced kindergarten teacher who has spent over eight years in the classroom. She focuses on creating a warm and inclusive atmosphere where every child feels valued and supported. Ms. Patel integrates art, music, and movement into her lessons to enhance learning and promote creativity. She holds a Master’s degree in Early Childhood Education and is dedicated to helping her students build a strong foundation for their academic journey while fostering a love for learning
